Our Company History

Founded in 2013 in Dubai, Horyal Trading Co LLC began as an export company facilitating trade between Dubai and East African countries, particularly South Sudan and Kenya. Over the years, our in-depth understanding of these markets and strong partnerships with local businesses have positioned us as a trusted trading intermediary.

While our roots are in export services, we've recognized the growing demand for quality agricultural products and have strategically expanded our business to focus on importing premium dry and perishable goods from South Sudan and Kenya to meet market needs.

  • Founded in Dubai in 2013

    Established with a vision to bridge trade opportunities between Dubai and East Africa through reliable export services and logistics expertise.

  • Strong Partnerships in South Sudan & Kenya

    A decade of building trust and developing reliable supplier relationships in key African markets, creating a sustainable trade ecosystem.

  • Evolution into Agricultural Imports

    Our business has evolved to meet changing market demands, now specializing in importing quality agricultural products while maintaining our trade expertise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our company, history, and operations

What is Horyal Trading Co LLC's background?

Horyal Trading Co LLC was established in Dubai in 2013 as an export company facilitating trade between Dubai and East African countries, particularly South Sudan and Kenya. Over the years, we've evolved to specialize in importing premium agricultural products while maintaining our core expertise in international trade and logistics.

Why did Horyal Trading shift focus to agricultural imports?

Our transition to agricultural imports was a strategic response to market demand and an opportunity to leverage our established trade networks in South Sudan and Kenya. These regions produce high-quality agricultural products that are in demand in various markets. Our decade of experience in international trade positioned us perfectly to facilitate the import of these products while ensuring quality and compliance with regulatory requirements.

Do you still offer export services?

While our primary focus has shifted to agricultural imports, we continue to support select export services for long-standing clients. Our expertise in international trade, logistics, and regulatory compliance remains available to businesses seeking comprehensive trade solutions. Please contact us to discuss your specific export needs.

How do you ensure quality in your agricultural imports?

Quality assurance is central to our operations. We implement rigorous supplier selection processes, regular quality checks at source, proper handling and storage during transportation, and final verification before delivery. For perishable goods, we maintain strict cold chain logistics. All our processes adhere to relevant international standards and regulatory requirements to ensure consistently high-quality products.

How can I partner with Horyal Trading Co LLC?

We welcome partnerships with both suppliers and buyers. For suppliers in South Sudan and Kenya, we offer access to international markets and logistics support. For buyers, we provide reliable sourcing of quality agricultural products. Contact us through our "Partner With Us" form or reach out directly via email or phone to discuss potential collaboration opportunities. We'll arrange a consultation to understand your specific needs and explore how we can work together.
