Our Journey

The evolution of Horyal Trading Co LLC from 2013 to present day

2013-2015

Foundation Years

Horyal Trading Co LLC was founded in Dubai with a vision to establish trade connections between the UAE and East African markets, particularly South Sudan and Kenya.

  • Established headquarters in Dubai
  • Developed initial export operations
  • Built first partnerships in East Africa
2016-2019

Growth Period

During this period of growth, we expanded our export portfolio and developed considerable expertise in international logistics and trade regulations.

  • Expanded export portfolio significantly
  • Developed deep logistics expertise
  • Strengthened regional partnerships
2020-2021

Transition Phase

Recognizing growing market opportunities in agricultural imports, we began a strategic transition period with extensive market research.

  • Conducted market research on agriculture
  • Developed agricultural import pilots
  • Created strategic business pivot plans
2022-Present

Agricultural Focus

Today, Horyal Trading Co LLC has successfully transitioned to focus primarily on agricultural imports from South Sudan and Kenya.

  • Full agricultural import operations
  • Cold chain logistics implementation
  • Expanded product portfolio development

"Our history in exports has given us unique insights and capabilities that now power our success in agricultural imports. This legacy of international trade excellence continues to be our foundation as we grow."

— Ahmed Hassan, Founder & CEO

Our Agricultural Evolution

How we've built upon our trading legacy to develop agricultural import excellence

Our trade experience taught us the importance of rigorous quality standards. For agricultural imports, we've developed specialized protocols for produce selection, handling, and transportation.

  • Farm-level quality inspections
  • Product-specific handling protocols
  • Specialized storage conditions

Cold Chain Logistics

Building upon our logistics expertise, we've invested in specialized cold chain infrastructure and procedures to maintain the freshness and quality of perishable agricultural products.

  • Temperature-controlled storage
  • Refrigerated transportation
  • Real-time monitoring systems
