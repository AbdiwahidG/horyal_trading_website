Product FAQs

Product FAQs

Common questions about our agricultural products and import services

What quality standards do your agricultural products meet?

All our agricultural products meet or exceed international quality standards relevant to their category. For food items, we adhere to HACCP principles and relevant ISO certifications. Our products also comply with UAE import requirements and specific industry standards for each product type. We conduct regular quality checks at multiple points in the supply chain to ensure consistency and excellence.

How do you ensure freshness for perishable goods?

We maintain freshness through a comprehensive cold chain logistics system. This includes temperature-controlled storage at source, refrigerated transportation, and proper handling procedures throughout the journey. Each perishable product is stored at its optimal temperature range, and we use advanced monitoring systems to track conditions during transit. Products are expedited through customs and delivered promptly to minimize time in transit, preserving quality and extending shelf life.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Our minimum order quantities vary by product category. For dry goods such as grains and pulses, minimum orders typically start at 100kg, while specialty items may have lower minimums. Perishable goods often have specific minimum quantities based on packaging units. We offer flexibility for regular clients and can accommodate different business needs. For detailed information on specific products, please contact our sales team who can provide current MOQ information and help structure orders to meet your requirements.

Do you offer product samples before bulk orders?

Yes, we provide product samples for qualified business clients considering substantial orders. Sample quantities and costs vary by product type. For dry goods, we can ship small sample quantities internationally. For perishable goods, sampling arrangements are typically made within the UAE to ensure freshness. Sample costs may be credited toward future orders, depending on the volume of subsequent purchases. Contact our team to arrange for product samples and sampling protocols.

Can you provide product certifications and documentation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive documentation for all our agricultural products. This includes certificates of origin, phytosanitary certificates for plant products, health certificates for animal products, and quality certifications relevant to each product type. For organic or specially certified products, we maintain complete chain of custody documentation. We also provide detailed product specifications and quality assurance reports upon request.
