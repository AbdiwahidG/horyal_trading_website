Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our agricultural imports and trade services

What agricultural products do you import?

We import a wide range of agricultural products from South Sudan and Kenya, categorized into dry goods (grains, pulses, nuts, spices) and perishables (fruits, vegetables, dairy, and meat products). All our imports undergo rigorous quality control to ensure they meet international standards.

How do you ensure quality for perishable agricultural imports?

We implement comprehensive cold chain logistics and quality assurance processes for all perishable goods. This includes careful selection of producers, proper handling and storage during transportation, temperature-controlled shipping, and final quality verification before delivery. We also adhere to all relevant food safety regulations and international standards.

Do you still offer the export services you provided in the past?

While our primary focus has shifted to agricultural imports, we continue to support select export services for long-standing clients. Our decade of expertise in international trade, logistics, and regulatory compliance remains available to businesses seeking comprehensive trade solutions. Please contact us to discuss your specific export needs.

What is the minimum order quantity for your agricultural imports?

We accommodate various order sizes to meet different business needs. For dry goods, minimum order quantities typically start at 100kg, while perishables often have lower minimums based on product type. For larger wholesale orders, we offer volume-based pricing. Please contact us for specific product minimum order quantities and pricing details.

How can I establish a long-term partnership with Horyal Trading Co LLC?

We welcome long-term partnerships and offer several collaboration models. Start by contacting us through our "Partner With Us" form, specifying your business needs and partnership goals. Our team will arrange a consultation to discuss potential collaboration structures, including regular supply agreements, exclusive product arrangements, or strategic alliances. We pride ourselves on creating tailored partnerships that deliver mutual value.
