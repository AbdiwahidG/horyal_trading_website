Contact FAQs

Contact FAQs

Common questions about connecting with Horyal Trading Co LLC

What's the best way to inquire about specific agricultural products?

For specific product inquiries, you can use our contact form above and select "Product Information" from the dropdown menu. Alternatively, you can email us directly at info@horyaltrading.com with details about the agricultural products you're interested in. For immediate assistance, you can also call our office during business hours. Please include as much detail as possible about your requirements, including product type, quantity, and timeline.

How can I become a supplier for Horyal Trading Co LLC?

We're always interested in connecting with quality agricultural producers in South Sudan and Kenya. To become a supplier, please use our contact form and select "Become a Supplier" from the dropdown menu. Our team will then reach out to discuss your products, quality standards, production capacity, and other relevant details. We look for suppliers who share our commitment to quality and can meet our documentation and certification requirements.

What information should I include in my initial inquiry?

To help us respond efficiently to your inquiry, please include: your name, company name (if applicable), contact details, the nature of your inquiry, specific products of interest, estimated quantities, intended use (retail, wholesale, processing, etc.), and your preferred timeline. For buyer inquiries, information about your business and requirements helps us provide tailored solutions. For supplier inquiries, details about your products, production capacity, and current certifications are valuable.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. Complex inquiries that require detailed product information, pricing, or logistics arrangements may take slightly longer as we gather the necessary information. If your inquiry is urgent, please indicate this in your message or call our office directly during business hours. Rest assured, every inquiry is important to us, and we'll do our best to address your needs promptly.

Do you have representatives in South Sudan and Kenya?

Yes, we maintain local representatives in both South Sudan and Kenya who work closely with our agricultural suppliers and handle local logistics arrangements. If you're located in either of these countries and wish to connect with our local representatives, please mention this in your inquiry, and we'll provide the appropriate contact information. Our local teams can assist with supplier relationships, quality assurance, and initial logistics coordination.
